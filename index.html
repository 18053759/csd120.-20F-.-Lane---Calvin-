Welcome to Calvin Lane's CSD120 Website!

My name is Calvin Lane, I am loving this opportunity to learn web design through programming and not a site-builder. I just completed High School at Superior Heights Collegiate and Vocational School and instantly moved into college to further my skills within programming. In my free time I create useless robots with arduino, play electic bass and guitar, and build/fix miscellaneous items. I want to be the kind of person who can give assistance to other people when they need it and try and be as nice as possible when meeting people. I aspire to do well in my classes and have a very enjoyable time in my freshman year of college!

I decided to add my second paragraph once I finished validating and fixing my code because I can add my thoughts on HTML as the course passes through! HTML is becoming more enjoyable as I learn the concepts, when I started this assignment I gave the instructions a quick read before doing the research, I will say that it seemd very intimidating. After finishing the code and learning what all of the concepts are and what everything does, I can look back on this as a very positive experience, I quite enjoy the formatting of HTML, its very user friendly.
